At B.S. Farm, we blend comfort with nature to offer a peaceful escape for families, couples, and nature lovers. Whether you're enjoying a quiet morning by the garden, swimming in the pool, or savoring freshly cooked meals, every moment here is designed to refresh your body and soul. Come experience the charm of Karjat with us!

Why Choose B.S.Farm

Nestled in lush greenery, B.S. Farm offers a peaceful escape from city life. It features an indoor swimming pool, garden spaces, and a multi-cuisine restaurant catering to all preferences. The air-conditioned cottages provide cozy comfort with all basic amenities. Located near Karjat Railway Station and scenic spots like Kothaligad Fort, it's perfect for weekend getaways. Ideal for families, couples, and nature lovers seeking serenity and fun.
